Introduction

Chemistry is the study of matter which is a general term for what forms all living and nonliving things. Chemistry is concerned mainly with the properties of matter. All of the items we encounter on a daily basis are made of matter.

  • Balloons are made of matter
  • Pancakes are made of matter

    • Atoms

    The fundamental building blocks of matter are atoms. The word atom comes from the Greek word atomos, which means "uncuttable". During 5th century the Greek philosophers proposed the idea that there must be a some smallest uncuttable units of matter. They called these units atoms.
